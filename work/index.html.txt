<section>
    <h2>Thickness Comparison</h2>
    <h3>25 mm vs. 75 mm Rigid Foam Sheets</h3>
    <h4>25 mm Foam Sheets:</h4>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Lightweight:</strong> Easier to handle and transport.</li>
        <li><strong>Cost-Effective:</strong> Generally less expensive.</li>
        <li><strong>Versatile Use:</strong> Suitable for applications where less insulation is needed.</li>
    </ul>

    <h4>75 mm Foam Sheets:</h4>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Superior Insulation:</strong> Higher thermal resistance for temperature-sensitive items.</li>
        <li><strong>Better Impact Protection:</strong> More cushioning for fragile goods during transport.</li>
        <li><strong>Soundproofing:</strong> Greater thickness enhances sound absorption capabilities.</li>
    </ul>
